About Us

Welcome to the AI-Powered Vehicle Recognition & Insights System, an innovative platform designed to revolutionize how users interact with vehicle data. Whether you're a car buyer, seller, enthusiast, or insurance assessor, our system empowers you to simply upload an image of a vehicle and receive detailed, real-time insights. From identifying the make and model to offering technical specifications, market prices, and common mechanical issues, our goal is to make vehicle information accessible, fast, and reliable.
